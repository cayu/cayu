## Conectores

- **SC** (Subscriber Connector) : EIA/TIA, FOCIS-3, IEC-61754-4.
- **LC** (Lucent Connector): FOCIS-10, EIA/TIA-604-10, IEC-61754-20.
- **FC** (Fixed Connector): FOCIS-4, EIA/TIA-604-04, IEC 61754-13.
- **ST** (Straight Tip): FOCIS-2, EIA/TIA-604-2.
